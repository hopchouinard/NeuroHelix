# Daily AI Research & Ideation Prompt Set

I. Research Prompts – “Fetch & Summarize”
 1. AI Ecosystem Watch
→ “Summarize the most important announcements in artificial intelligence from the last 48 hours. Focus on model releases, new toolkits, and research milestones.”
 2. Tech Regulation Pulse
→ “Identify any new AI-related laws, bills, or policy statements from North America or the EU in the last 7 days and summarize potential business impacts.”
 3. Emergent Open-Source Activity
→ “List and briefly describe new open-source AI frameworks, libraries, or datasets released this week.”
 4. Hardware & Compute Landscape
→ “Track recent updates in GPU, TPU, or edge AI hardware and summarize which technologies could influence LLM deployment costs.”
 5. Ethics & Alignment
→ “Scan recent think-tank or academic discussions on AI alignment, safety, or governance and summarize key perspectives.”

⸻

II. Tool & Market Tracking – “Monitor & Compare”
 6. Model Comparison Digest
→ “Compare any benchmark results or performance discussions between major models (Gemini, GPT-4, Claude 3, Mistral, etc.) published in the last week.”
 7. Corporate Strategy Roundup
→ “Summarize notable partnerships, acquisitions, or strategic moves by top AI vendors.”
 8. Startup Radar
→ “List emerging startups or open-source communities developing notable AI applications, particularly agentic frameworks.”
 9. Developer-Tool Evolution
→ “Report any new features or releases for GitHub Copilot, Claude Code, Cursor, or similar AI-assisted coding environments.”
 10. Prompt-Engineering Trends
→ “Identify new prompt-engineering techniques or frameworks discussed by leading practitioners online.”

⸻

III. Ideation Loop – “Generate & Extend”
 11. Concept Synthesizer
→ “From today’s research summaries, infer 5 potential project or product ideas that could be built within the next 12 months.”
 12. Novelty Filter
→ “Evaluate yesterday’s top 5 ideas and rank them by novelty and technical feasibility using today’s research as evidence.”
 13. Continuity Builder
→ “Compare the last 7 days of idea summaries to detect recurring patterns or emerging meta-themes.”
 14. Meta-Project Explorer
→ “Based on accumulated daily reports, suggest how the system itself could improve its data collection or synthesis pipeline.”

⸻

IV. Creative & Analytical Reformatting – “Reframe & Render”
 15. Cross-Domain Insight
→ “Identify connections between developments in AI, biotech, and quantum computing that could inspire cross-disciplinary research.”
 16. Market Implication Lens
→ “For each major AI announcement, summarize who benefits, who loses, and which industries are disrupted.”
 17. Visualization Prompt
→ “Create an outline for an HTML dashboard section summarizing today’s insights, including possible charts, color palettes, and key metrics.”
 18. Narrative Mode
→ “Write a 200-word narrative brief summarizing today’s AI landscape as if for an executive newsletter.”

⸻

V. Meta-Maintenance & Evolution
 19. Prompt-Health Checker
→ “Review all current daily prompts for redundancy or drift. Suggest which ones should be rephrased, merged, or retired.”
 20. New-Topic Detector
→ “From trending discussions, propose 3 new research categories worth adding to the daily loop.”
